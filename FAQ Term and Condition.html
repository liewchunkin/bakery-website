Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver?
Yes,our deliver area is on all of the city in West Malaysia.The prices are based on post code start from RM5 per thing that deliver.

How to pay?
We accept PayPal, Bank Transfer, Cheque and Cash. Full payment is required before delivery/collection. For tiered cakes and wedding cakes a 25% deposit is required upon ordering, the amount is dependant on each creation.

How do i get promotion?
You can get promotion by becoming one of our member with sign up an persona account.

How long will your bakery food keep?
We advise that our bread be eaten on the day of delivery,however,Celebration and wedding cakes should keep for about 5 days. We advise that our cakes ahould not be refrigerated as this might damage the fondant icing and sugar work. Our cakes should be stored in the box provided, in a cool dry place.
